RA-006: Attack Drone Live Fire

Restricted / Active
Case ID: Oppgave 2 (Forsvaret)
Operation Type: Specific / Military Test
Payload: NAMMO Warhead (Live)
Location: Active Firing Range (Skytefelt)

1. Concept of Operations

Live fire testing of a Norwegian-supplied attack drone against armored targets. The drone has undocumented specifications and open air intakes. Warhead is equipped with a custom "Safe & Arm" device.

2. Critical Information Needs (Uncertainties)

  • Reliability: Probability of unintended detonation if drone crashes?
  • FTS: Does the drone have an independent Flight Termination System?
  • Safe & Arm: What are the specific arming criteria (altitude, speed, manual)?
  • IP Rating: Verification of IP rating vs open air intakes (moisture/dust ingress risk).

3. Hazard Log (Extract)

ID Hazard Cause Mitigation (Safety Barriers) Residual Risk
H-01 Unintended Detonation Faulty Safe & Arm unit, vibration, or hard landing.
  • Safe Zone: Only essential personnel (bunker).
  • Arming: Only arm immediately before launch.
  • Verify: Technical inspection of S&A unit before each flight.
 HIGH
H-02 Fly-away (Lost Control) Navigational error or loss of link causing drone to leave range.
  • Geofence: Hard limit set well within range boundaries.
  • Energy Limit: Fly with 30% fuel to ensure physical inability to reach populated areas.
  • Independent FTS (Kill switch).
 MEDIUM
H-03 System Failure (Env) Water/dust ingress via open intakes causing short circuit.
  • Limit: Fly only in VMC (Visual Meteorological Conditions), no rain.
  • Visual inspection of intakes pre-flight.
 MEDIUM
H-04 Emergency (Injury) Accident during handling or testing.
  • ERP: Designated Scene Commander & Medic.
  • Evacuation vehicle (Sanitet) on standby.
  • Pre-briefed route to hospital.
